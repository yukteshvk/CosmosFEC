With 32 cosmic bowling lanes and comfortable leather lounging areas, Cosmos Family Entertainment is perfect for both friends and family to enjoy. Cosmos Bowling leagues range from kids to adults. Be sure to contact us if you wish to enter!

Laser Tag

Cosmos Family Entertainment features a 3,500-square-foot, 2-story, Laser Tag arena. It is a must play attraction where you can battle against your friends, family, or other opponents in captivatingly colorful settings. Laser tag missions are 15 minutes in length and start every 20 minutes.


Cost: $7.99 per person per round

Cost: $9.99 per person
